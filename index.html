Projects

Phonology of Garo

I investigated the acoustics of word prosody of Garo in my MA at Calgary under the supervision of Dr. Angeliki Athanasopoulou. In my PhD (which is ongoing), I have mostly focused on the phonological side of prosodic words in Garo. I analyzed the progressive gemination process and word internal phonotactics for my first QP. For my second QP, I analyzed the rhythmic deletion of the glottal stop in Garo.
