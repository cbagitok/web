About Me

Hi, welcome to my personal website. I am currently a PhD student in Linguistics at McGill University. I do most of my research in the area of theoretical phonology, with occasional forays into theoretical syntax. I am mostly interested in prosodic structures and how they are mapped from the syntactic module. You can find the materials related to my research under the Research tab.
